Full and Half Day Trainings

Learn from the Best

From our start with Capital Camp to the iterations of Drupal Government Days to the newly branded Drupal GovCon, we've worked to provide amazing cutting edge training and this year is no different. This year, we have expanded training offerings to all three days of the Con at the NIH main campus.

Not only is the Drupal GovCon training offered by leaders in Open Source and/or Drupal training, it is an amazing offering for all skill levels and provide hands-on real-world examples and practice.Training class sizes are limited. So, you will need to sign up for the training using the links provided to each of the training classes.

We want this to be a great experience for our trainers and attendees, so please, Help Us out and ONLY register for trainings you will attend and is appropriate for your individual skill level.  Where necessary, also please come to the class prepared with any required software installed and properly configured. Note: These ARE hands-on training, so don't forget your wifi-enabled laptop. Usually, no special software is needed but where it is, the individual training partner will let you know. Additionally, you may use PC or Mac.

Please note, you must be registered for Drupal GovCon in order to attend ANY TRAINING sessions.  So, not sure you have a ticket? Just login. When logged in, the registration page lists your tickets, including tickets you've requested for others. Same as your user "Tickets" tab.

Can't login? Try requesting a new password or try searching for yourself on the community page. You can search by first name or last name.
