Business and Strategy

The purpose of this track is two-fold - one angle helps you to understand the advantages of Drupal from an organizational leadership angle and the other is to highlight the great Drupal sites and projects you're working on and to show what's possible. These sessions are business focused, and will be non-technical.

Integrating 3rd party API’s with Drupal

Description: Drupal has a great structure but what happens when you need more than Drupal can provide? IYoutube, Twitter, and Amazon AWS Services are just some of the API options to bring in fresh, dynamic content into your site. This session will introduce some practical methods which help deliver new and trendy content to users.

Working with nodes in bulk

Description: Views Bulk Operations (VBO) is a great module for performing operations on groups of nodes.

 

What you will learn: In this session you will learn how to create custom dashboards using views as well as configuring VBO.  Using VBO we will show how to update the content of many nodes with a single action

 

Intended Audience: This session will be useful for both beginning and intermediate audiences.  Basic knowledge of views will be helpful, but some intro for beginners will be provided.

Connecting the dots (the improvements) and aligning them (to achieve the website goals) are sometimes the biggest struggle for government websites. How best can we use data to discover and then prioritize our updates for the most effective improvements?
