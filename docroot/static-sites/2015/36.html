Where to Stay

Drupal GovCon 2015 Hotel Room Block


We sold out the Room block at the Doubletree. We have negotiated a new room block at the hotel right next door for anyone who hasn't booked yet.

We have a room block at The American Inn of Bethesda located at 8130 Wisconsin Avenue, Bethesda, MD. When booking, to receive the conference block rate, enter the GROUP CODE of 150720Drup. This rate is $162 and includes continental breakfast, WIFI, 24 hour Business Center and Fitness center, newspaper and shuttle service (Monday-Friday). The hotel block expires JULY 13th. 

For more information about the rate or if you have additional questions, please email Jared King at jking with DRUPAL in the subject line. 

The Double Tree and the American Inn of Bethesda are both on the free NIH shuttle route, are only 5 blocks from the downtown Bethesda metro and most of all, both hotels are within walking distance to our venue!

If you have questions about the room blocks at the American Inn of Bethesda or the Doubletree, please email Jared King at jking
