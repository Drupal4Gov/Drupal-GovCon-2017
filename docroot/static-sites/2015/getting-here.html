Traveling to Bethesda? We Have a Drupal GovCon Official Hotel!

We are excited to announce that Drupal GovCon has a block of rooms at the DoubleTree Bethesda Hotel, July 20 - July 24.
 
The room rate is $162 per night for single or double occupancy rooms. This includes free WiFi, outdoor pool and double Hilton Honors points.  

To obtain this rate and reserve your room at the DoubleTree Bethesda, please email Andrew Reader at abpr with DRUPAL in the subject line. This rate is not available directly through the DoubleTree. You must use Yeego Travel Meetings + Events for this rate.
