Networking

Networking is a big part of what we do at Drupal GovCon. It gives attendees the opportunity to get to know each other and foster community growth and empowerment.

When attending a Social Networking Event, please keep in mind that we have a Code Of Conduct and we expect all attendees at both the Drupal GovCon official events and non official events hosted by our partners and sponsors (listed below) to adhere to that Code of Conduct. Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in an attendee being banned or expelled from the conference or the after hours networking event.

YES, WE ARE SERIOUS. 

So, go read the Code of Conduct and then go network with the amazing community brought together at Drupal GovCon.
